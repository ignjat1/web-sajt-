Revuelto

The Lamborghini Revuelto is a mid-engine plug-in hybrid sports car produced by the Italian automobile manufacturer Lamborghini. It was officially unveiled on March 29, 2023, as a successor to the Aventador. The car will go into production in the spring of 2023, with the first units expected to be delivered in the last quarter of 2023.

Specifications

  • Engine: 6.5 L V12
  • Horsepower: 814 hp
  • Top Speed: 350 km/h
