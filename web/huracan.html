Huracan

The Lamborghini Huracán (Spanish for hurricane) is a sport car manufactured by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini replacing the previous V10 offering, the Gallardo. The Huracán was revealed online in December 2013, making its worldwide debut at the 2014 Geneva Auto Show, and was released in the market in 2014.

Specifications

  • Engine: 5.2L V10
  • Horsepower: 602 hp
  • Top Speed: 341km/h
