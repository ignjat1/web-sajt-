Aventador

The Lamborghini Aventador is a mid-engine sportscar produced by the Italian autmotovie manufacturer Lamborghini. In keeping with Lamborghini tradition, the Aventador is named after a Spanish fighting bull that fought in Zaragoza, Aragon, in 1993.
